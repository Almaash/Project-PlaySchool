About Us

The Gingerbread House

We are excited to have you join our community of learning and exploration. At The Gingerbread House, we strive to create an environment where children feel welcomed, secure, and excited about the possibilities and opportunities for learning.

Our curriculum is designed to meet the individual needs of each child and to foster the development of skills that will help them succeed in school and beyond. We provide a variety of opportunities for children to learn and grow, including field trips, hands-on projects, art, music, and outdoor play.

At The Gingerbread House, we believe that learning should be an enjoyable experience. Our teachers and staff are passionate about creating a safe, supportive, and engaging environment for children to learn and grow. We look forward to having you join us at The Gingerbread House and helping to create a bright future for our children.

Our Mission


The mission of pre-school is to provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for young children to learn and grow. Pre-school programs focus on providing activities and experiences that help children develop the social, emotional, cognitive and physical skills they need to be successful in school and in life. Pre-school programs strive to foster a love of learning and exploration.


Our Vision


To provide an educational experience that encourages children to develop socially, emotionally, intellectually, and physically in a safe, secure and nurturing environment. We strive to engage each child in meaningful learning experiences that are developmentally appropriate and culturally relevant, empowering them to become independent, responsible, and confident learners.
